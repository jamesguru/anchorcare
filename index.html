Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our disability support services

What services does Care Anchor provide?

We offer mentoring, support work, community access, recovery coaching, and support coordination services for NDIS participants in Northern Adelaide and surrounding areas.

How do I know if I'm eligible for your services?

If you have an NDIS plan that includes funding for the services we provide, you can access our support. We also offer free initial consultations to discuss your needs and eligibility.

What areas do you service?

We primarily service Northern Adelaide and surrounding areas in South Australia. However, we can provide some services remotely throughout SA depending on your needs.

How much do your services cost?

Our services are funded through your NDIS plan if you have the appropriate supports included. There are no out-of-pocket costs for participants with relevant funding in their NDIS plans.

Can I change support providers if I'm not happy?

Yes, you have complete choice and control over your supports. If you're not satisfied with your current provider, you can change to a different provider at any time.

How quickly can I start receiving services?

Once we've completed our initial assessment and service agreement, we can typically begin providing support within 1-2 weeks, depending on your specific needs and availability.
